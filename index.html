Upcoming & Recent Sessions

Getting To Know Us

Every 3rd Wednesday

We meet once a month to share our knowledge and experiences to learn and discuss how to improve collaboration between teams and leverage automation to build better businesses. Typically our meetings last 1-2 hours, and are centered around talks that show off new tool, techniquess and companies journeys. Topics generally flow into open discussion at the end.

This is a group targeted to engineers (both dev and ops), tech professionals, managers, leads, architects and entrepreneurs coming together to learn about and share information on DevOps processes, tools, practices and benefits.

We maintain a welcoming and friendly group for everyone. Please read our Code of Conduct

What is DevOps?

A cultural and professional movement, focused on how we build and operate high velocity organizations, born from the experiences of its practitioners.
